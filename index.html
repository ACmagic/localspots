Frequently Asked Questions

How does pre-purchasing a gift card help out my local spots?

Australian businesses are currently experiencing huge declines in customer numbers due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Pre-purchasing gift cards gives businesses a boost of much-needed cash flow to cover fixed costs (think rent, bills and employee wages). Together we can help these businesses stay afloat until they can get back to business as usual.

How do I get my business listed on LocalSpots?

Create an account with us, then send us an email at localspots.com.au@gmail.com and we'll verify your identity so that you can list your business.

I’ve purchased my gift card, how do I use it?

Your gift card will become active 90 days after you purchase it, and will remain valid for 3 years. Simply present your confirmation email or the order number messaged to you and proof of ID at your local business to redeem.

I own a business listed on LocalSpots, how do I claim it?

Send us an email at localspots.com.au@gmail.com and we'll help you claim it and your funds. We can also help you create your own account so you manage your own payments.

Does LocalSpots charge any fees?

We’re a not for profit initiative however, LocalSpots charges a 3% fee on each transaction with a minimum transaction fee of 50c to cover payment processing platform and server hosting fees to keep this website running!

How does LocalSpots verify business owners claiming a LocalSpot?

We verify the owner’s business registration information and only pay out to bank accounts registered to the business owners. Once a business is verified, they can withdraw funds from gift card purchases for their business on LocalSpot.
